## Typography

### Primary typeface: Aktiv Grotesk

Authoritative yet neutral. Flexible family with 24 styles (Hairline to Black). Primarily use Light, Regular, and Medium.

**Fallback:** Neue Haas Grotesk Text Pro Roman

| Usage | Weight | Line height | Letter spacing |
|---|---|---|---|
| Headlines | Light | 130% | 0% |
| Sub header / body text | Light | 145% | 0% |
| Box text | Regular | 160% | 0% |
| Small texts | Regular | 160% | 0% |
| Buttons | Medium | 130% | 5% |
| Text links | Medium | 160% | 5% |

### Secondary typeface: PP Neue Machina

Monospace/geometric. Used sparingly (~30% ratio to Aktiv Grotesk). 'Plain Regular' fontstyle.

**Fallback:** Neue Haas Grotesk Text Pro Bold

| Usage | Case | Line height | Letter spacing |
|---|---|---|---|
| Highlighted keywords in big titles | Normal | 130% | 0% |
| Highlighted numbers | Normal | Varies | 0% |
| Quotes | Normal | 130% | 0% |
| Alternate short titles | Normal | 160% | 0% |
| Tags & markers | UPPERCASE | 130% | 20% |

### Font ratio rule

Aktiv Grotesk should always be the dominant typeface — **70/30** split with PP Neue Machina.

## Icons

### Stroke icon style

- Custom stroke design with soft corners
- Stroke colour: Purple `#5500FF`
- Service icons (large): stroke weight 1.5
- Resource icons (small): stroke weight 1

### Graphic elements

Shapes from the winning behaviour icons and the 'c' from the ampersand can serve as decorative elements. Use in all primary palette colours.

---

## Photography style

- Urban, cool, and authentic
- Everyday, unposed moments
- Natural, warm light
- Real people — friendly and casual
- City environment backgrounds with architectural details
- Images always have rounded corners (40px desktop, 30px mobile)
- No overlays / tints for readability — reposition text instead
- Never place logo on top of people from chest up

| Background | Logo version |
|---|---|

| Dark Grey, Black | White (one-colour) |
